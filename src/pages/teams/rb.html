Foundations as Toro Rosso and AlphaTauri

Visa Cash App RB’s roots trace back to 2006, when the team was originally founded as Scuderia Toro Rosso. Toro Rosso served as Red Bull Racing’s junior team, providing a platform to develop young talent. Drivers like Sebastian Vettel, Daniel Ricciardo, and Max Verstappen began their careers with Toro Rosso, making it a key stepping stone in Formula One. In 2020, the team rebranded as AlphaTauri, aligning with Red Bull’s fashion brand. This era emphasized style, innovation, and competitiveness, with Pierre Gasly securing an unforgettable victory at the 2020 Italian Grand Prix.

Rebranding to Visa Cash App RB
