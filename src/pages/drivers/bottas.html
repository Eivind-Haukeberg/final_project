A Promising Beginning

Valtteri Bottas, born on August 28, 1989, in Nastola, Finland, showcased his talent early in karting before making waves in single-seater racing. After winning the Formula Renault Eurocup and Formula Renault Northern European Cup in 2008, he established himself as one of the brightest prospects in motorsport. His success in the GP3 Series in 2011 cemented his reputation, earning him a spot in Formula One with Williams in 2013.

Success with Mercedes

Bottas joined Mercedes in 2017, stepping into one of the most competitive seats on the grid. Over his five seasons with the team, he contributed significantly to their dominance, helping secure multiple Constructors' Championships. Bottas claimed 10 Grand Prix victories and consistently finished at the sharp end of the championship standings, proving himself as a reliable and fast driver alongside Lewis Hamilton.

A New Chapter

In 2022, Bottas embarked on a new chapter with Alfa Romeo (formerly Sauber), bringing experience and leadership to the midfield team. Known for his calm demeanor and calculated driving, Bottas remains a key figure on the grid. His strong racecraft and resilience ensure that he continues to be a respected competitor, inspiring fans and fellow drivers alike.
