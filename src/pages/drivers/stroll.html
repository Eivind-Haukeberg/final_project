A Rapid Rise to Formula One

Born on October 29, 1998, in Montreal, Canada, Lance Stroll quickly made a name for himself in motorsport, progressing through karting and junior categories with notable success. As the 2016 FIA Formula 3 European Champion, Stroll's impressive performances earned him a Formula One debut with Williams in 2017. At just 18 years old, he became the youngest driver to secure a podium in his rookie season, finishing third at the Azerbaijan Grand Prix.

A Key Figure at Aston Martin

Stroll transitioned to Racing Point, now Aston Martin, in 2019, becoming an integral part of the team's journey in Formula One. His 2020 season was a highlight, achieving a pole position at the Turkish Grand Prix and multiple podium finishes.
